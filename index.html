POPULAR PACKAGES

TOSH-KUTLA

The Magical Getaway

No roads, no network, getting back to the true roots of humans, only you and the nature is a serenity, that describes Kutla. A getaway from the urban hustle and bustle, Kutla is situated above Tosh village, a trek of 1 hour. Perfect place to calm your soul and find solidarity, the truly Magical Getaway.

LEH LADAKH

Wanderlust Nomad

When you itch for freedom of getting away for a while to discover there's a world away from your world, the Leh Ladakh travel route is what you truly yearn. Travelling across the highest Highway in India, crossing the polar deserts gives the satisfaction you deserve of being our Wanderlust Nomad.

LAHAUL & SPITI

The Snow Valley

Since the opening of the Atal Tunnel, Lahaul & Spiti has become easily accessible for every person. Experiencing the culture of the locals and expanding your horizon (especially in the winters!) has never been easier. Welcome to the true Snow Valley.

MANALI

Perfect Family Escape

If you want to enjoy the urban culture of the mountains while surrounded by the most beautiful ranges and valleys without much travelling lag, Manali is the place for you! With its family friendly, beautiful and non-tiring trips, Manali easily becomes the hotspot for both new and veteran travellers making it the Perfect Family Escape.

CHANDERTAL LAKE

The Moon Lake

Chandertal is a tourist destination for trekkers and campers. Surrounded by vast meadows, carpeted with hundreds of wild flowers, it justifies its myth of the lake where Gods Dance at Night, The Moon Lake.

KASOL

Hippie Paradise

The base point for all travel destinations in the Parvati Valley, Kasol shines as the modern culture knitted within its roots. Perfect place for starting any of your journey, Kasol hides near the Parvati river, engulfed by forests and becoming the true Hippie Paradise.
